## Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM)

## Project Overview

Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM) is a data-driven statistical framework designed to quantify the contribution of various marketing channels toward business outcomes such as sales, conversions, revenue, and customer acquisition. The solution enables organizations to evaluate marketing effectiveness, measure channel-level ROI, and optimize media budget allocation across both online and offline channels.

The platform combines econometric modeling, statistical analysis, and predictive analytics to help marketing teams make informed investment decisions and maximize return on marketing spend (ROAS).

---

## Business Problem

Organizations invest significant budgets across multiple marketing channels including TV, Digital, Search, Social Media, Display, Influencer Marketing, CRM, and Offline Campaigns. However, understanding the true impact of each channel on business performance remains challenging.

Common business questions include:

- Which marketing channels contribute most to sales and conversions?
- How much revenue is generated by each channel?
- What is the optimal media budget allocation?
- Which channels are experiencing diminishing returns?
- How can overall marketing ROI be improved?

Without a robust measurement framework, marketing decisions are often driven by assumptions rather than data, resulting in inefficient spending and missed growth opportunities.

---

## Solution

Developed an end-to-end Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM) framework to quantify the effectiveness of marketing investments and identify optimal budget allocation strategies.

Integrated data from multiple marketing channels, sales systems, customer touchpoints, and external business factors. Performed extensive exploratory analysis, feature engineering, adstock transformations, seasonality adjustments, and multicollinearity assessments to prepare the data for modeling.

Built and evaluated Bayesian Regression and Linear Regression models to estimate channel-level contribution, incremental sales impact, media effectiveness, and return on investment. Generated scenario planning and budget optimization recommendations to support strategic marketing decisions.

The solution enabled stakeholders to understand the true drivers of business growth and optimize marketing investments based on data-driven insights.

## Results & Evaluation

### Model Performance

- Achieved model explanatory power (R²) of approximately **82%** across marketing and sales datasets.
- Quantified contribution of individual marketing channels toward overall business outcomes.
- Identified diminishing returns and saturation points across key media investments.
- Improved accuracy of marketing performance measurement compared to last-click attribution approaches.

### Business Outcomes

- Identified opportunities to improve marketing ROI by approximately **18%** through optimized budget allocation.
- Enabled more effective distribution of media investments across high-performing channels.
- Improved visibility into channel-level performance and contribution.
- Supported data-driven marketing planning and investment decisions.
- Reduced reliance on subjective marketing performance assessments.

### Technical Achievements

- Developed a scalable MMM framework supporting multiple marketing channels and business objectives.
- Implemented adstock transformations and seasonality adjustments to capture delayed marketing effects.
- Built scenario simulation capabilities for budget planning and optimization.
- Created executive dashboards for monitoring channel performance, ROI, and investment effectiveness.
